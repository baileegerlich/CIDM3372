Bailee's Weekly Workout Routine

Finding the time to exercise and get my body moving and increasing my heart rate is very important to me. I try to workout as much as 5 times a week. However, my schedule is very busy some days so a 30 minute workout is all I can do. I mainly focus on low impact cardio and heavy weight lifting. That is just what works for me and my body genetics.

    My Weekly Workout Split:
  • Monday: cardio and upperbody - chest and shoulders

  • Tuesday: legs - glutes and quads

  • Wednesday: cardio, back and, abs

  • Thursday: legs - glutes, hamstrings, and calves

  • Friday: cardio, abs, and upperbody - chest and back

  • Saturdays and Sundays: active rest days/ recovery days
    • On my active rest days I try go for walks, do yoga, or deep stretching

Below are specific movements I like to do

Lower Body
  1. Hip thrusts
  2. Barbell back squats
  3. Romanian deadlifts
  4. Split squats
  5. Single leg RDLs
  6. Hip abductors

Upper Body
  1. Bench press
  2. Shoulder press
  3. Skull crushers
  4. Bicep curls
  5. Barbell rows
  6. Face pull on the cable machine

For abs I like to use YouTube videos by Pamela Reif.
I also use YouTube videos for yoga. I recommend @yogawithadriene and @yogawithbird.
    For cardio:
  • Walking on the treadmill - speed: 3mph and incline: 7-12%
  • Pilates
  • Stationary Bike
  • Stair Master
  • HIIT
    • Once again I use YouTube for pilates and HIIT workouts
