What Bailee Gerlich Eats



I do not follow any specific diet or restrict foods. I believe in eating everything you want, but in moderation. I try to eat as many whole foods as possible, but my taste buds are picky. Thus my food options are minuscule.

  • Breakfast: In the morning, I like to eat avocado toast with scrambled eggs. Another go to are waffles with a bowl of mixed barries.

  • Lunch: I tend to eat cafe style foods such as sandwiches, soup, or salads. Sometime my dad and I will walk to our favoirte Thei food restaurant and I will order fried rice or noodles.

  • Dinner: For dinner I love cooking pastas with salon or chicken and grilled veggies on the side

  • Snacks: I like to eat greek yogurt cups, fruits that are easy to throw in my backpack like apples, oranges, and bananas.
